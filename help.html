Daily Health Tips

Simple ways to improve your wellbeing

💧

Stay Hydrated

Drink 8-10 glasses of water daily to maintain healthy blood pressure and support medication effectiveness.

🚶‍♂️

Regular Exercise

Aim for 30 minutes of moderate exercise daily. Walking, swimming, or cycling can improve heart health.

🥗

Balanced Diet

Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Limit processed foods and excess sodium.

😴

Quality Sleep

Get 7-9 hours of sleep nightly. Good sleep helps regulate hormones and supports immune function.

🧘‍♀️

Stress Management

Practice meditation, deep breathing, or yoga to reduce stress and improve mental health.
